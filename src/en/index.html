Hello there, my name is Rodrigo Núñez. I live in A Coruña, Galicia, Spain. I’ve worked for twelve years as a graphic designer, web designer, and front-end developer. Both as a freelancer and within a digital marketing agency. I’ve been involved in all kinds of projects for a wide range of clients, taking part in every phase of the process. I’ve led the design department, handling everything from online marketing campaigns to the development of websites for both national and international clients. What defines me: flexibility, a collaborative spirit, and a curious mind. I'm used to working with the latest technologies and keep learning every day. I believe content comes before form, and that design is, above all, a tool for communication. I like to listen, pay attention to detail, and deliver work on time.
